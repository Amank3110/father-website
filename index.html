ABOUT US

Laying the Groundwork for Greatness

UniQuest Institute is a premier coaching centre located in the heart of Patiala, dedicated to guiding students from Class 6 through 12 toward their academic and competitive examination goals.

Our experienced faculty brings clarity to complex concepts, fostering both academic excellence and intellectual curiosity. We believe every student carries immense potential — our role is to build the foundation that helps them reach it.

Admissions Process

A simple, transparent process to begin your journey towards excellence.

01

Enquire

Contact us via phone, email, or visit us in person at our Patiala centre to learn about available programs.

02

Assessment

Appear for a brief diagnostic test to help us understand the student's current level and place them in the right batch.

03

Counselling

One-on-one session with faculty to discuss goals, program selection, batch timings, and fee structure.

04

Enrol & Begin

Complete the registration formalities and commence your structured learning journey at UniQuest.

📋

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

Previous class report card · Passport-size photographs · Aadhar Card copy · Parent/Guardian contact details. Seats are limited — early registration is strongly advised to secure your place in preferred batches.
